About UnBroke



Unbroke is a web application that aims to create a platform that connects small local businesses to students.
UnBroke provides:

  • small businesses who strive to collect customers and students who wish to save
    money on food, UnBroke Food provides a platform to connect.
  • provides the technological resources, for businesses to advertise coupons and
    promotions, that areeasily accessible to students.
  • students with up-to-date information on local eateries for a budget-friendly price.
